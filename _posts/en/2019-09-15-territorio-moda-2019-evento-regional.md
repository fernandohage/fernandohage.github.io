## About the Project

Launch of the clothing pre-collection created by the designers who sell at **Espaço Moda – São José Liberto** (Belém-Pará), as part of the **Fashion Territory** project by the Amazon Gem and Jewelry Institute.

## My Role

I acted as a consultant on the project during the first half of 2019, providing guidance and monitoring for the research, planning, development, and production of the product collection.

## Participating Brands

The following brands participated in the consultancy:

- **TuCrias** (Renata Gonçalves)
- **Prado** (Tiago Prado)
- **Fabio Purificação**
- **Madame Floresta** (Graça Arruda)
- **Miçante** (Rafaela Silva)
- **Patchouli da Amazônia** (Andressa Sarmanho)
- **Ludmila Heringer**
- **Murilo Maia**
- **Lilia Lima**
- **Helena Bezerra**

## Photoshoot Context

The photos featuring the clothing items were taken for the launch of the **"Inner Times"** accessories collection, coordinated by designer Yorrana Maia, for an exhibition held in September 2019.

## Credits

- **Fashion Production (Photos)**: Diogo Carneiro
- **Photography**: Luan Neves
- **Models**: Marília Moutinho and Beatriz Ornela
- **Production Assistant**: Joyce Ramalho

## Project Importance

The **Fashion Territory** project represents an important initiative for the development of Amazonian fashion, connecting local designers and promoting the value of regional production. The consultancy provided specialized technical support for enhancing the collections and strengthening the participating brands.
